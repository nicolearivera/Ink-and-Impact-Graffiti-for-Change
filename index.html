Welcome to Ink & Impact: Graffiti for Change

Discover how street art is transforming communities and inspiring social change.

Goal of this Webpage

To explore graffiti as a powerful form of artistic expression and social commentary that transforms public spaces, amplifies marginalized voices, and fosters dialogue around identity, inequality, and activism. This discussion will examine how graffiti can be used as an educational tool to promote critical thinking, cultural awareness, and student engagement with real-world social issues through art.

What is graffiti?

  1. Words, figures, and images created on public/private surfaces without permission.
  2. A form of artistic and cultural expression.

Dual Perception of Graffiti

  • Viewed as art by some, vandalism by others.
  • Reflects cultural narratives and social issues
